CONCACAF CHAMPIONS CUP Messi Starts Against Nashville SC in Crucial Round of 16 Tie

Lionel Messi is scheduled to start for Inter Miami against Nashville SC tonight, Wednesday, March 18, 2026, at Chase Stadium. He is currently in good form, having been rested recently for an MLS match to ensure maximum fitness for this critical second-leg cup tie.
